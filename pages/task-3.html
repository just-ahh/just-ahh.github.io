Narrative

What I want people to get out of this website?

  • To know how to view games in a different light.
  • Understand different examples of video games in museums.
  • Show adults there is a different way to enjoy museum content.

Navigation

Thinking of navigation in terms of having only a few pages available, not trying to draw attention away from the information.

  1. Opening on a homepage explaining the definition of games and benefits of them in learning.
  2. Goes next to a page on adults and learning through games and playing.
  3. Having a navigation bar that has links to separate sections on examples of games and places to visit.
  4. Next going to examples of games aimed at all ages / a middle aged demographic.
  5. Another page to discuss benefits of these games.
  6. A page for quick facts (maybe cut due to time constraints)
  7. And finally a contact page

Independent Learning

Persona 1

Name Lorraine
Age 55
Occupation Customer Advisor in Retail
Web Habits Basic Online order, asks children for help if anything doesn't work
Quotes "I don't play video games, online bingo does not count."
Knowledge Level Thinks video games are just for teenage boys, filled with violence.
Goals To understand why people of all ages would play video games, see them used in a learning context.
Needs / Wants Easy Layout, more mature layout and design.
Skills Can work websites, might need prompts for any complicated actions.
Narrative Wants to understand her children and grandchildren's reactions to video games and their use in learning when she takes the grandkids to the museum on a day out.

Persona 2

Name Mark
Age 45
Occupation Oil Rig Worker
Web Habits Looks at news and Facebook for social media, plays games on his phone but won't admit they are video games.
Quotes "I saw it on Facebook, of course it's true."
Knowledge Level Games are for children, adults won't benefit from them.
Goals Understand that games are not just for children and that they take many forms.
Needs / Wants Not too complicated design and content, but enough to retain attention.
Skills Lots of practical skills but slower on gaining digital skills.
Narrative Access the website to see what all the fuss is about on these games.

My audience reflects my narrowed down choice of user. It also makes my choices clearer in what features would be best to use and language. The audience would need a clean layout without too many external and distracting features, especially audio and video. But these could be incorporated at a later date if I feel they could enhance user experience and not hinder it. Text will need to be to the point, without a lot of rambling. Making sure it is understood by the age range and doesn't come off as condescending is top priority for the information dissemination.

My Website Mood Board

My mood board is a current guide on how I want my website to look. I have decided on Helvetica for the font and what I think is an okay colour palette. It goes between both video game and museum iconography, including the idea of learning because that is the main message of the site. I believe the font is accessible and easy to read. With my colour palette chosen for background and also shading to really make some sections pop. With my plan to include my own images from research trips to my chosen museums, the imagery will change dependent on what I capture.
